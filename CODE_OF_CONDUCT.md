# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, technically rigorous community. Participate in good faith, focus
criticism on ideas and evidence, respect privacy boundaries, and do not harass, threaten, demean, or
discriminate against contributors.

Do not pressure anyone to disclose private identity, employer, customer, security finding, or data.
Do not post credentials, customer content, exploit details before coordinated disclosure, or other
material that creates avoidable risk.

Maintainers may edit or remove content, reject contributions, restrict participation, or ban users
whose conduct threatens people, the project, or responsible vulnerability handling. Report conduct
issues privately through repository moderation tools. Security vulnerabilities must use the process
in `SECURITY.md`.
